Customer Testimonial

I recently tried the pineapple gouda pizza from Cheech's and it exceeded my expectations. The sweet and tangy pineapple paired perfectly with the creamy and savory gouda cheese. The crust was crispy and the toppings were piled high, giving each slice a generous amount of flavor. The only downside was that the pizza was a bit greasy, but it didn't take away from the overall deliciousness. I definitely recommend giving the pineapple gouda pizza a try at Cheech's!

I recently ordered the feta and field greens pizza from Cheech's and I have to say, it was absolutely delicious! Everything from the crust to the toppings was perfect and the flavors blended together so well. The feta cheese added a nice tangy taste and the fresh field greens gave it a healthy touch.

I ordered a pizza from Cheech's and unfortunately, it wasn't what I wanted. However, when I called them to inform them about the mistake, they were polite and quick in rectifying the issue. They replaced the pizza for me and encouraged me to try the erronius pizza. You know what? That mistake was absolutely delicious! I am giving them 5 stars for their amazing customer service and for turning the mistake into a tasty surprise. I will definitely be ordering from them again.
